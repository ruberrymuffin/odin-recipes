Pasta Recipe

A simple elegant meal that is easy to put together

  • Pasta (1 cup)
  • Frozen shrimp
  • Pesto sauce
  • Frozen veggies
  • 1 ripe avocado

Steps

  1. Boil water with a pinch of salt
  2. Once it's bubbling add uncooked pasta
  3. While the pasta is cooking, cut and cube avocado
  4. Prepare a sauce pan with pesto sauce and add frozen veggies
  5. Stir pasta and pesto as needed
  6. When pasta is almost done, add frozen shrimp
  7. Add/mash in avocado to pesto sauce
  8. When pasta is cooked; strain out water
  9. Add sauce to pasta
  10. Enjoy!
