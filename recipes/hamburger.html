Hamburger Recipe

My favorite food

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • 1 frozen patty (veggie or otherwise)
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Sauces of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook the patty in the airfryer for the designated amount of time
  2. Broil the bun in the oven to your liking
  3. After taking out the bun add sauces to the inside of the bun on both sides
  4. Once the patty is done, add it to the bottom bun
  5. Add lettuce and onions
  6. Enjoy!
