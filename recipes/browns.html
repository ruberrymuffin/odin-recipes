Hashbrowns and Eggs Recipe

A quick and easy meal that great's for breakfast, lunch, or dinner

Ingredients

  • Frozen bag of hasbrowns (1 cup)
  • 2 eggs
  • Frozen bag of cubed/chopped veggies of your choice (1 cup)
  • 1 ripe avocado

Steps

  1. Cook hasbrowns in a pan with some oil on medium heat until slightly brown
  2. Add veggies to the pan with the hashbrown until veggies and browns are fully cooked
    • Any veggies of your choice
    • The more the merrier!
  3. While the pan is cooking, cut and cube avocado
  4. Once veggies and browns are done; add to a bowl and add the cubed avocado on top
  5. On the empty pan cook eggs over easy or to your liking
  6. Add eggs to bowl and season/sauce as desired
  7. Enjoy!
